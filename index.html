Services

At Phindulo Cleaning Services, we provide a full spectrum of cleaning solutions, from residential to commercial. Our expert team uses advanced techniques and eco-friendly products to ensure outstanding results. Whether for routine maintenance or a deep clean, we guarantee exceptional quality and a spotless environment.

Residential Cleaning

Comprehensive cleaning for homes, including routine housekeeping, deep cleaning, and specialized tasks such as carpet and upholstery cleaning.

Commercial Cleaning

Professional cleaning for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial environments, with services ranging from daily maintenance to periodic deep cleaning and sanitation.

Post-Construction Cleaning

Thorough cleaning of newly constructed or renovated spaces to remove dust, debris, and residues, ensuring the space is ready for occupancy.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning

Detailed cleaning services designed to prepare a property for new occupants or to ensure a smooth transition when moving out, including deep cleaning of all surfaces and fixtures.
